# Demand Radar — Goal

## North star

Surface painful, repeated business workflows where money is already moving — so that productionize_engine can sell a paid manual pilot before any SaaS is built.

## V1 success — one real opportunity brief in 4 weeks

Within 4 weeks of starting V1, produce **one** opportunity brief I would actually act on.

A brief counts as "done" when it contains all of:

- **≥ 15 unique evidence URLs** drawn from **≥ 4 different subreddits** in the watchlist
- **≥ 3 verbatim buyer quotes** (their exact words, not paraphrased)
- **≥ 3 named competitor weaknesses** tied to specific tools (e.g. "HubSpot — too expensive for < 10-seat agencies")
- A drafted **$299–$500 manual offer** with a concrete weekly deliverable
- A scorecard total **≥ 35 / 45** per `productionize_engine/docs/OPPORTUNITY_SELECTION.md`
- A buyer persona where I can plausibly find 100 prospects on LinkedIn / Clutch / a directory

## What this goal is NOT

It is not "build the perfect signal pipeline." It is "find one opportunity worth pursuing this month, with evidence good enough to write outreach against."

## Anti-goals — do not drift into these

- **No web dashboard.** Markdown + CSV reports only.
- **No automated Reddit DMs, posting, or voting.** Read-only forever.
- **No second data source** until the first brief from Reddit is in hand.
- **No theme-clustering with embeddings** until ≥ 500 signals exist.
- **No keyword-filter tuning past ~70% accuracy** — that is the LLM's job, not regex's.
- **No "more subreddits"** — the 48-row watchlist is the budget. Add only if a brief explicitly demands a new community.
- **No premature productization** — do not write a backend abstraction, dependency injection layer, or generic SDK for any of this until it has paid for itself.

## Why this goal is the right shape

- *Specific*: one brief, with measurable evidence thresholds.
- *Achievable*: 4 weeks is enough for ETL + classification + one brief, and not enough for distractions.
- *Falsifiable*: at week 4 either the brief exists and passes the scorecard, or it doesn't.
- *Aligned with the larger loop*: demand_radar exists to feed productionize_engine, which exists to ship a paid pilot. One usable brief unblocks the next stage.
